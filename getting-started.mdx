## Step 1: Create your account

1. Go to [app.optima-voice.com](https://app.optima-voice.com)
2. Click **Sign Up** in the top right
3. Choose your sign-up method:
   - **Email & Password** (traditional)
   - **Google** (fastest)
   - **GitHub** (for developers)

<Tip>
We recommend Google or GitHub sign-up for the fastest onboarding experience.
</Tip>

4. Verify your email (if using email sign-up)
5. You'll be redirected to your dashboard

---

## Step 2: Set up your organization

After signing up, you'll create your first organization:

1. Enter your **Organization Name** (e.g., "Acme Corp", "Smith & Associates")
2. Choose your **Industry** from the dropdown
3. Click **Create Organization**

<Note>
Your organization is your workspace. You can invite team members, manage billing, and configure settings here. You can create multiple organizations if needed.
</Note>

---

## Step 3: Create your first agent

Now let's create an AI voice agent:

1. Click **"Create Agent"** button on your Agents page
2. Choose from three creation methods:

### Option A: Quick Setup (Recommended)

AI-powered agent creation - fastest way to get started:

1. **Business Name**: Your company name
2. **Business Description**: Tell us what you do (2-3 sentences)
   - Example: "We're a dental office offering cleanings, fillings, and cosmetic dentistry. We're open Mon-Fri 9AM-5PM."
3. **Agent Goal**: What should this agent handle?
   - Example: "Answer patient questions, book appointments, provide pricing"
4. Click **"Generate Agent"**

Our AI automatically creates:
- Professional greeting message
- Conversation instructions tailored to your business
- Appropriate tone and personality

<Tip>
The more specific your business description, the better your agent performs. Include hours, services, and key policies.
</Tip>

### Option B: Use Template

Pre-built agents for common industries:

1. Browse templates for:
   - Medical/Dental offices
   - Restaurants
   - Retail stores
   - Professional services
   - And more
2. Select a template
3. Customize to your business
4. Deploy immediately

### Option C: Build Custom

Full manual control for advanced users:

1. **Agent Name**: Name your agent
2. **Voice Instructions**: Write detailed behavior instructions (50-2000 characters)
   - What topics to handle
   - How to respond
   - What NOT to do
3. **Greeting Message**: Opening message callers hear (20-200 characters)
4. Click **"Create Agent"**

---

## Step 4: Choose a voice

Your agent needs a voice! Optima Voice offers 100+ professional AI voices:

1. On the agent configuration page, find the **"Voice"** section
2. Use the filters to narrow your search:
   - **Language**: English, Spanish, French, and 50+ more
   - **Gender**: Male, Female, or All
   - **Search**: Type a voice name or description
3. Click the **play button** (â–¶) to preview any voice
4. When you find one you like, select it

<Tip>
**Pro tip**: Match the voice to your brand. A law firm might choose a calm, authoritative voice, while a kids' store might choose something energetic and friendly.
</Tip>

---

## Step 5: Get a phone number

Your agent needs a number for customers to call:

### Purchasing a number (Recommended)

1. Go to **"Phone Numbers"** in the sidebar
2. Click **"Add Number"**
3. Choose **"Purchase from Telnyx"** tab
4. Select your **Country**
5. Browse available numbers or search by area code
6. Click **"Purchase"** on your preferred number ($1-5/month depending on location)

### Using your existing number

Already have a business number? You can forward it to Optima Voice:

1. Choose **"Manual Entry"** tab
2. Enter your existing phone number
3. We'll provide forwarding instructions for your carrier

---

## Step 6: Assign the number to your agent

Connect your phone number to your agent:

1. On the **Phone Numbers** page, find your new number
2. Click **"Assign to Agent"**
3. Select your agent from the dropdown
4. Click **"Assign"**

Your agent is now live and ready to receive calls!

---

## Step 7: Make a test call

Time to test your agent:

1. From your agent's page, click the **"Test"** button in the header
2. You'll see a test widget with:
   - Your agent's assigned phone number
   - A **"Call Now"** button (requires browser permissions)
3. Click **"Call Now"** to test in-browser, OR
4. Call the number from your phone

<Tip>
First calls can take 2-3 seconds to connect as the agent initializes. Subsequent calls connect instantly.
</Tip>
