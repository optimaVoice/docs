## Overview

Phone numbers are how customers reach your AI agents. Optima Voice makes it easy to purchase new numbers or bring your existing ones.

**Two options:**
1. **Purchase from Telnyx** (recommended) - Instant provisioning, $1-5/month
2. **Manual Entry** - Use your existing business number

---

## Purchasing a Number (Recommended)

### Step 1: Open Add Number Drawer

1. Navigate to **Phone Numbers** in the sidebar
2. Click **"Add Number"** button
3. Select the **"Purchase from Telnyx"** tab

### Step 2: Search for Numbers

**Choose your country:**
- Select from 75+ supported countries
- Popular: United States, Canada, United Kingdom, Australia
- Each country has different pricing ($1-5/month typically)

**Optional: Filter by area code**
- Leave blank to see all available numbers
- Enter specific area code for local presence
  - Example: "212" for New York City
  - Example: "415" for San Francisco
  - Example: "020" for London

**Click "Search"**

### Step 3: Browse Results

You'll see up to 20 available numbers:

```
+1 (555) 123-4567  |  Local  |  $2.00/month  |  [Purchase]
+1 (555) 234-5678  |  Local  |  $2.00/month  |  [Purchase]
+1 (555) 345-6789  |  Toll-Free | $5.00/month | [Purchase]
```

**Number types:**
- **Local:** Standard area code numbers
- **Toll-Free:** 800, 888, 877, etc. (higher cost, professional appearance)

<Tip>
**Local numbers** are better for regional businesses. **Toll-free** numbers work well for national companies and add credibility.
</Tip>

### Step 4: Purchase & Assign

1. Click **"Purchase"** on your preferred number
2. Confirm purchase (charges immediately)
3. **Assign to Agent** dialog appears
4. Select which agent should receive calls to this number
5. Click **"Assign"**

**Done!** Your number is live immediately.

## Manual Entry (Existing Numbers)

Already have a business number? Forward it to Optima Voice.

### Step 1: Enter Number Details

1. Click **"Add Number"** button
2. Select **"Manual Entry"** tab
3. Fill in:
   - **Name:** Identifier (e.g., "Main Reception Line")
   - **Phone Number:** Your existing number (format: +1234567890)
   - **Agent:** Which agent handles this number

### Step 2: Configure Outbound (Optional)

If your agent needs to make outbound calls:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Outbound Configuration (Advanced)">
    Enable outbound calling to let your agent initiate calls:
    
    **Required fields:**
    - **Outbound Address:** Your SIP provider's address
    - **Auth Username:** SIP account username
    - **Auth Password:** SIP account password
    
    **Optional settings:**
    - **Transport:** AUTO (recommended), UDP, TCP, TLS
    - **Media Encryption:** DISABLE (default), SRTP
    - **Krisp Noise Cancellation:** On/Off
    
    <Note>
    Most users don't need outbound. This is for advanced call center setups.
    </Note>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Step 3: Set Up Call Forwarding

After adding your number to Optima Voice:

1. **We'll provide SIP credentials** (displayed after setup)
2. **Configure your carrier** to forward calls to our SIP address
3. **Test the connection** by calling your number

<Warning>
Manual setup requires technical knowledge of SIP/VoIP. Most users prefer purchasing numbers directly through Optima Voice for instant setup.
</Warning>

## Managing Phone Numbers

### Assigning to Agents

Each number can be assigned to one agent at a time.

**To assign:**
1. Find the number in your Phone Numbers list
2. Click **"Assign to Agent"**
3. Select agent from dropdown
4. Click **"Assign"**

**To reassign:**
1. Click **"Reassign"** on the number
2. Select different agent
3. Click **"Confirm"**
4. Previous agent stops receiving calls immediately
5. New agent starts receiving calls

<Note>
Reassigning is instant. Active calls continue with the old agent, but new calls route to the newly assigned agent.
</Note>

### Unassigning Numbers

Remove an agent from a number without deleting it:

1. Click on the phone number row
2. Click **"Unassign"**
3. Confirm

**Result:** Number stays in your account but doesn't route to any agent. Callers hear a generic message.

<Warning>
Unassigned numbers still incur monthly charges. Delete them if you don't plan to use them.
</Warning>

### Deleting Numbers

**Purchased numbers:**
1. Click **"Delete"** on the number row
2. Confirm deletion
3. Number is released back to Telnyx
4. Monthly charges stop at end of billing cycle

**Manually entered numbers:**
1. Click **"Delete"**
2. Confirm
3. Number is removed from Optima Voice
4. Your original carrier still has the number (set up forwarding elsewhere)

<Warning>
**Deletion is permanent.** If you delete a purchased number, you cannot guarantee getting the same number back. Someone else might claim it.
</Warning>

## Testing Your Numbers

After purchasing or configuring:

### Quick Test

1. Call the number from your phone
2. Verify:
   - ‚úÖ Call connects within 2-3 seconds
   - ‚úÖ Agent greeting plays
   - ‚úÖ Agent responds to questions
   - ‚úÖ Audio quality is clear (no static, echoes)

### Troubleshooting Call Issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Call doesn't connect">
    **Possible causes:**
    - Number not assigned to agent
    - Agent is not configured (missing voice, instructions)
    - Provisioning still in progress (wait 2 minutes after purchase)
    
    **Solutions:**
    - Check Phone Numbers page - is number assigned?
    - Check agent settings - is everything configured?
    - Try again in 2-3 minutes
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Audio quality is poor">
    **Possible causes:**
    - Network congestion
    - Low bandwidth
    - Krisp noise cancellation off
    
    **Solutions:**
    - Enable Krisp Noise Cancellation in number settings
    - Check your internet connection (agents need 1+ Mbps)
    - Try calling from different location/network
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Agent doesn't respond">
    **Possible causes:**
    - Agent instructions are empty/too short
    - Voice model not selected
    - Backend service issue
    
    **Solutions:**
    - Check agent Voice Instructions (minimum 50 characters)
    - Verify voice is selected in agent settings
    - Contact support if issue persists
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Calls drop after X seconds">
    **Possible causes:**
    - Max Call Duration limit reached
    - Agent explicitly ended call
    
    **Solutions:**
    - Check agent Advanced Settings ‚Üí Max Call Duration
    - Review call transcript to see if agent ended it intentionally
    - Increase Max Call Duration if needed
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Best Practices

### Number Selection

‚úÖ **DO:**
- **Choose memorable numbers** when possible (repeating digits, patterns)
- **Match area codes** to your business location for local credibility
- **Use toll-free** for national brands or premium services
- **Purchase multiple** if you have multiple agents (reception, sales, support)

‚ùå **DON'T:**
- Use vanity numbers with letters (hard to remember: 1-800-FLOWERS vs 1-800-356-9377)
- Buy numbers in random cities (looks unprofessional if customer is in New York and sees Los Angeles number)
- Reuse personal mobile numbers (keep business and personal separate)

### Assignment Strategy

**Single Agent:**
- Purchase 1 number
- Assign to your only agent
- Simple setup, one point of contact

**Multiple Agents:**
- Purchase 1 number per department:
  - +1 (555) 100-0001 ‚Üí Sales Agent
  - +1 (555) 100-0002 ‚Üí Support Agent
  - +1 (555) 100-0003 ‚Üí Billing Agent
- Advertise different numbers for different purposes

**High Volume:**
- Purchase multiple numbers for the same agent
- Distribute to different marketing channels
- Track which channel drives calls (see call logs)

## Number Portability

### Bringing Your Existing Number

Want to transfer your current business number to Optima Voice?

**Process:**
1. **Contact Support:** Email support@optima-voice.com with:
   - Current phone number
   - Current carrier name
   - Account number with current carrier
2. **Submit Port Request:** We'll handle the paperwork
3. **Wait for Approval:** 7-14 business days (carrier dependent)
4. **Number Transfers:** Seamless cutover, no downtime

<Note>
**Port-in fees:** $15-25 per number (one-time). Monthly billing remains $1-5/month.
</Note>

### Taking Your Number Elsewhere

Want to leave Optima Voice but keep your number?

**Process:**
1. **Request Port-Out:** Contact support@optima-voice.com
2. **We'll Provide:** Account details, port authorization code
3. **Submit to New Carrier:** They'll handle the transfer
4. **Number Transfers:** 7-14 business days

<Warning>
Don't delete the number in Optima Voice until the port completes. Deletion makes the number unportable.
</Warning>

## FAQs

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I have multiple numbers for one agent?">
    Yes! Assign as many numbers as you want to a single agent. Useful for tracking marketing campaigns or providing multiple local numbers.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What happens if I don't assign a number to an agent?">
    Callers hear a generic message: "This number is not configured. Please contact the business directly." You still pay monthly fees.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I transfer a number between agents instantly?">
    Yes. Reassignment takes effect immediately. Active calls continue with the old agent; new calls go to the new agent.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Do I own the number I purchase?">
    You lease the number from Telnyx. As long as you pay monthly fees, it's yours. If you cancel, the number returns to the pool.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I get a specific number I found elsewhere?">
    Maybe. If it's available in our Telnyx inventory, yes. Search for it. If not found, it's already claimed.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What's the difference between Local and Toll-Free?">
    - **Local:** Tied to geographic area code (e.g., 212 for NYC). Cheaper ($1-2/month).
    - **Toll-Free:** 800/888/877 numbers. National reach. More expensive ($5/month). Professional appearance.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
